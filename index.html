The Rochester NY Chaper of the American Statistical Association

Our work

Our work is focused upon statistics education, developing coding experience,and free scientific consultation regarding the proper use of statistical analysis. We are primarily a self-organized learning community founded upon improving our ability to understand and employ modern data science analysis and programming.

Our group

Our group consists of local students, university faculty and math teachers working in and around Rochester and western NY. We welcome all participants.

How to join our group?

contact Dr. Ernest Fokoue at the school of Mathematical Science at the Rochester Institute of Technology .
